ALVO - VIRTUAL REALITY SHOOTER

Live the battlefield in this immersive VR shooter

ALVO is 5v5 online shooter developed for PSVR and Oculus. It features three game modes: Free4All , Search and Destroy, and Team DeathMatch. Customize your guns, gear and character with a variety of unique skins and equipment.

Game Modes & Maps:

  • Team Deathmatch
  • Search and destroy (TBC)
  • 7 maps, including day/night variations.
